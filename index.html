AI Legal Contract Extractor

A full-stack legal document review system. Ingests PDF, DOCX, HTML, and TXT contracts, runs a multi-model AI pipeline (Gemini 2.5 Flash → Grok → DeepSeek fallback) to extract structured fields with citations and confidence scores, then presents results in an interactive review table with CSV/Excel export. Includes OCR for scanned PDFs and Docker support for one-command deployment.
